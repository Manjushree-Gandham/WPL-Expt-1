The Home page serves as the central access point of the City Transport Information System and provides an overview of the portal. Displays detailed information about available transport routes including route numbers, source, destination and status. Lists all stops covered under a selected route in a structured manner along with nearby landmarks. Provides a visual representation of transport routes and major stops with interactive map access. Shows route-wise daily transport schedules including arrival and departure timings. Allows citizens to submit feedback, suggestions or complaints related to transport services. Provides links to transport authority websites, emergency helplines and downloadable timetables.
